2024 NASA Space Apps Challenge

Navigator for the Habitable Worlds Observatory (HWO): Mapping the Characterizable Exoplanets in our Galaxy

To observe the possible habitable exoplanets that can be seen and characterized by the HWO, hover over the telescope slider on the top right corner of your screen and change the possible diameter of the lens to be used. To get more information about each exoplanet or star, hover with your mouse over it and see the details come to life! The small green dot in the middle is planet Earth and all of the exoplanets are referenced to it. Enjoy your space endeavour!
